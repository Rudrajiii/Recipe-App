# Nutrition Analysis Prompt

Here is a recipe for samosas, along with the approximate nutritional information for one samosa:

Ingredients:

* 2 cups all-purpose flour
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
* 1/2 cup warm water
* 1 cup mashed potatoes
* 1/2 cup green peas
* 1/2 cup diced onions
* 1/2 cup diced carrots
* 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
* 1/2 tsp coriander powder
* 1/2 tsp garam masala
* 1/4 tsp turmeric
* 1/4 tsp chili powder
* 1/4 cup vegetable oil
* Salt to taste

Instructions:

1. To make the dough, combine the flour and salt in a large bowl. Add the ghee and mix until it resembles coarse breadcrumbs. Gradually add the warm water and knead until a smooth dough forms. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
2. To make the filling, heat the oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the cumin seeds and onions and cook until the onions are translucent.
3. Add the carrots, coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
4. Add the mashed potatoes, green peas, and salt. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until heated through.
5. Divide the dough into 8-10 balls. Roll each ball into a circle. Cut the circle in half to form a semi-circle.
6. Spoon some filling onto the semi-circle, leaving a border around the edges. Fold the edges over the filling to form a triangle. Seal the edges by pressing them together with a fork.
7. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the samosas until they are golden brown. Drain on paper towels.

Approximate Nutritional Information per Samosa:

* Calories: 250
* Protein: 5g
* Fat: 13g
* Carbohydrates: 32g
* Sugar: 2g

Note: Nutritional information may vary depending on the specific ingredients and brands used. This is approximate and for informational purposes only.
