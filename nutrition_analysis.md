**Teriyaki Chicken Recipe**

Ingredients:

* 500g boneless, skinless chicken thighs
* 1/4 cup soy sauce
* 2 tablespoons mirin
* 2 tablespoons sake
* 2 tablespoons honey
* 1 tablespoon grated ginger
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 tablespoon cornstarch
* 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
* Sesame seeds and sliced green onions, for garnish

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, mirin, sake, honey, ginger, and garlic.
2. Place the chicken in a large zip-top bag and add the marinade. Seal the bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
3. Remove the chicken from the marinade and let it come to room temperature. Reserve the marinade.
4. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
5. Heat the vegetable oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
6. Sear the chicken on both sides until browned, about 2-3 minutes per side.
7. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
8. While the chicken is cooking, pour the reserved marinade into a small saucepan and bring it to a boil.
9. In a small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and a splash of water. Slowly whisk the cornstarch mixture into the boiling marinade until thickened.
10. Once the chicken is cooked, let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
11. Serve the chicken with the teriyaki sauce, sesame seeds, and sliced green onions.
